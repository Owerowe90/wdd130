Types of Flies

Fish look for different types of flies during different times of the year There are three different categories of flies that you need to know. The wet, dry and streamer flies: Wet flies are supposed to imitate the nymph form of insects and are supposed to float down in the water at various depths. They sink. Dry flies are imitating the insects as they emerge from the depths and start to dry off to fly away and mate. The fish look for them floating on the surface and eat them. They float. Streamers are flies that are to get the attention of the fish and normally are bigger flies. They are flashy and can look like a small fish, leeches or other large aquatic animals. They are technically wet flies, but deserve their own category.
